Introduction

We provide funding to help you research and develop new products and services, purchase capital equipment, safeguard jobs, sort your waste and recycling, develop new products and services, improve productivity and access new markets.
We also work with the private sector to provide attractive equity investment to help you move to your next stage of growth.

The range of funding we provide includes:

  • Regional Selective Assistance (RSA)
  • R&D grants
  • Assistance with co-investment

Tell us about what you need, and we’ll tell you what funding would be most suitable for you.
